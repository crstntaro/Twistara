Why Twistara?

The Power of One Brush

The All-in-One Detachable Makeup Brush offers consumers a convenient, space-saving, and travel-friendly solution to their beauty routine by combining multiple brush heads into a single, compact tool.

Designed for versatility, it allows users to easily switch between brushes for foundation, blush, eyeshadow, and more — making it ideal for both quick touch-ups and full makeup applications.

This innovative tool not only reduces clutter in makeup kits but also promotes hygiene through easy cleaning and storage. Perfect for on-the-go individuals, it ensures that beauty enthusiasts can maintain a polished look anytime, anywhere without carrying multiple bulky brushes.

Production Process

  • Bamboo Preparation, Cleaning and Cutting
  • Bristle Preparation and Cutting
  • Drilling and Shaping
  • Combining the Bristle and Bamboo parts of the Detachable Heads
  • Cleaning and Shining of Make-Up Brush Handle and Detachable Heads
  • Checking Product Quality and Packaging
