About the Company

Twistara was created through a partnership among five visionary entrepreneurs: Keith Docado, Jhanella Domingo, Steve Enriquez, Hanz Esmeria, and Ariane Gabata.

Each member brings unique expertise and skills to the company, contributing to its overall growth and success...

The name “Twistara” blends “twist” (creativity) and “aura” (natural beauty).

Through its All-in-One Detachable Makeup Brush, Twistara offers travel-friendly, eco-conscious beauty tools.

Our Mission

To create innovative beauty solutions that enhance confidence, promote sustainability, and make routines joyful and effortless.

Our Vision

To be a leading name in the beauty tool industry through eco-friendly, convenient, and stylish solutions.
